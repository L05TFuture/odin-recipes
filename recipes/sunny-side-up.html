Sunny Side Up

Ingredients

  • 1 teasspoon olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instrcutions

  1. Heat the oil in a medium nonstick skillet over low heat until slightly shimmering, about 5 minutes.
  2. Crack an egg into a small ramekin and slowly add it to the skillet; repeat with the other egg, adding it to the other side of the skillet.
  3. Cover with a tight lid and cook, uninterrupted, until the whites are completely set but the yolks are still runny, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes.
  4. Slide the eggs out of the skillet onto a plate or toast.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
